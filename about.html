About us

  • Kartikay is a dedicated and passionate seller of Buddha rice. Kartikay has always had a deep appreciation for the culture and traditions of his country. He was inspired to become a seller of Buddha rice after learning about the health benefits and cultural significance of the grain.
  • Kartikay takes great pride in his work and is committed to providing his customers with the highest quality Buddha rice available. He works closely with local farmers to ensure that only the best crops are selected for sale, and he personally inspects each batch of rice before it is packaged and shipped to customers.
  • Despite the challenges of running a small business, Kartikay remains positive and enthusiastic about his work. He is always looking for new ways to improve his product and better serve his customers. With his passion, dedication, and hard work, Kartikay is sure to continue to be a successful seller of Buddha rice for many years to come.
