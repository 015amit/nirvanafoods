Benefits of Buddha Rice

  • Low glycemic index ( 49% - 52%): Kalanamak rice has a low glycemic index (GI), which means it is slowly digested and absorbed, leading to a slower rise in blood sugar levels. This makes it a good option for people with diabetes or those looking to manage their blood sugar levels.

  • Rich in minerals: Kalanamak rice is a good source of essential minerals such as iron, zinc, and magnesium, which are important for maintaining healthy bones, skin, and hair.

  • High in protein: It has 11% protein, almost double that of common rice varieties.

  • High in antioxidants: Antioxidants have anti-inflammatory properties and may help reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as cancer and heart disease.

  • Good source of fiber: Kalanamak rice is high in dietary fiber, which is essential for maintaining healthy digestion and preventing constipation.

  • Gluten-free: Kalanamak rice is naturally gluten-free, making it a good option for people with gluten intolerance or celiac disease.

  • Aromatic and flavorful: Kalanamak rice has a distinctive nutty and earthy flavor. Its unique aroma also enhances the overall flavor and taste of the dishes.

cooking instructions

  • Rinse the rice thrice with regular water to get rid of any excessive starch.
  • Soak the rice in twice the amount of water for fifteen minutes.

Open pot method

  • Bring a pot of water (five times the amount of rice) to a boil. Add a pinch of salt and one tbsp of oil.
  • Add the soaked rice and bring it to a boil. After boiling, reduce the flame.
  • Cook for ten to thirteen minutes.
  • Strain out the excess water and let the rice rest for three minutes before serving.

Closed pot method

  • Bring a pot of water (two and a half times the amount of rice) to a boil. Add a pinch of salt and one tbsp of oil.
  • Add the soaked rice and bring it to a boil agian and reduce the flame.
  • Cover the pot with a lid and cook the rice for ten to thirteen minutes.
  • Fork the rice and serve hot.

Microwave method

  • Add water (two and a half times the amount of rice) to a microwave safe bowl and add the soaked rice.
  • Add a pinch of salt and one tbsp of oil & stir.
  • Cook it at 180 celsius for ten to thirteen minutes.
  • Fork the rice and serve hot..
